Detailed Description

template<class Vector>
class rtl::IA_Segmenter< Vector >

Segmenter class for partitioning of continuous stream of points clusters.

The abbreviation stands for:

  • Incremental - segments continuous stream of points
  • Adaptive - point neighbourhood scales with distance from the origin.

The IA_Segmenter continualy accepts points through the addPoint() method and sorts them into continuous clusters. The criterion of continuity is the proximity test as follows:

  • Distance threshold is computed - it is proportional to the scale factor given by setScaling() and a distance of the point of interest from origin (specified in loadData() second parameter).
  • Distance threshold is clipped by the lower and upper bounds specified during construction or explicitly by setLowerBound() or setUpperBound().
  • Required number of neighbours is examined and if a point-neighbour distance is lower than the threshold, the are set to belong to the same cluster.
  • If one point can belong to more clusters, the clusters are merged.
  • If the point does not have any close enough neighbour, a new alive cluster is created.
  • The cluster expanded with a new point the longest time ago is checked and is its last update happened setStep() points before, it is moved from alive clusters to closed clusters and cannot be expanded any more.
