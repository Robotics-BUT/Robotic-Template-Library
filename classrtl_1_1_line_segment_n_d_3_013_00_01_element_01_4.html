Construction from both end points and a corresponding direction vector.

Care must be taken to to provide a true direction vector (E - B).normalized(). No validity checks are performed and member function may lead to unexpected results with wrong D. This constructor is meant to reduce overhead in specific cases, where D arise as a side effect of other computation. For regular sitation use the end-point-only constructor.
