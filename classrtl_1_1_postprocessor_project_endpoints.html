Detailed Description

template<class Approximation>
class rtl::PostprocessorProjectEndpoints< Approximation >

Trims approximations with their end points to produce constrained output primitives.

All approximations are given by a set of points in a limited area, but are infinite in some direction (lines in one, planes in two etc.). End-point projection limits infinite approximations to appropriate constrained types (line segments, polygons) covering only the area of interest.
